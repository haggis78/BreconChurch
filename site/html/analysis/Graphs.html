Variations per Anonymous Block

The graph shown below shows the number of variations per "anonymous block," which is similar to a paragraph in our document. In other words, this graph shows the number of <app> elements that were found in each block.

In the future, the project team aims to adjust this graph to be more accurate in its findings. As of right now, we were unable to take into account the varying lengths of anonymous blocks, which would have affected the proportions of variations that are graphed. Rather than using proportions, the team used the count function, which has skewed our results slightly.

As it stands, however, the graph shows that there are significantly more variations in anonymous blocks 6 and 13. This information will allow us to study which changes were considered significant to the editors and writers of future Charters.
