Welcome to the Brecon Project!

This is a digitally-based project that uses XML to encode the documents related to the foundation of Brecon Collegiate Church and School in 1541. The project also aims to perform a manuscript collation on the varying copies of the official government copy of the Letter Patent detailing the move of the Collegiate Church.

For more information about the history of Brecon College, or to learn more about our project, visit the About tab.
